About Rickie Lee Reeves

The Prolific Writer and Lyricist

My name is Rickie Lee Reeves. I am a prolific writer and lyricist of songs for many different genres. I have been doing this for over 40 years.

My mission is to be recognized for my great lyrics. I am currently exploring options to sell my lyrics to artists and producers and will update this section as I finalize the best path forward.

Stay tuned for updates on how to license and collaborate on these songs!
